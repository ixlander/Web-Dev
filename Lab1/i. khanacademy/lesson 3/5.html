Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour1 1/2 cups
Baking powder3 1/2 teaspoons
Salt1/4 teaspoon
Sugar1 tablespoon
Milk1 1/4 cups
Egg1
Melted butter3 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

Step 2: Make a well in the center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.

Step 3: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.

Step 4: Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

Step 5: Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Fettuccine pasta8 oz
Butter1/4 cup
Garlic, minced4 cloves
Heavy cream1 cup
Grated Parmesan cheese1 cup
SaltTo taste
Black pepperTo taste
Fresh parsley, chopped2 tablespoons

Step 1: Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 3: Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.

Step 4: Add grated Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and smooth.

Step 5: Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6: Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the sauce.

Step 7: Garnish with chopped parsley and serve immediately.

Berry Blast Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)2 cups
Banana1
Greek yogurt1/2 cup
Milk (dairy or plant-based)1 cup
Honey1 tablespoon
Ice cubes1 cup

Step 1: Add all ingredients to a blender in the order listed.

Step 2: Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, about 1-2 minutes.

Step 3: Taste and adjust sweetness with more honey if needed.

Step 4: Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
